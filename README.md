# 3rd-p-Equipment
Update and partial rewrite of grassman's [Third Person Equipment Mod](https://github.com/Vermintide-Mod-Framework/Grasmann-Mods/tree/master/third_person_equipment) for Vermintide 2.

Description :
Shows your equipment on your character.
Melee- and ranged weapon as well as tomes, grims, potions, bombs, heal items and equiped trinket.
Works for yourself, other players and bots
